### Notes

Asked the same question (what is a JS variable) three times with three different roles assigned. No role: got a generic textbook definition, accurate but flat. Patient teacher role: got the 'labeled jar in your kitchen' analogy, 3 sentences, no jargon. Senior tech writer role: got a mini docs page with headers (Definition, Syntax, Example, Common Mistake), and it covered `const` vs `let` vs `var` plus the hoisting gotcha. The writer's was strongest because it included a real bug example.

### What I learned

Role is a filter. Same question, but the role decides what is in scope and how it gets framed. Patient teacher for new concepts. Tech writer for things I am trying to nail down.
